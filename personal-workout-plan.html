PROTOCOL NOTES

  • Warm-up — 5 min light cardio + dynamic stretches before every session
  • Compound ramp — empty bar × 10 → 50% × 5 → 70% × 3 → 85% × 1 → working sets
  • RIR target — 1–2 reps in reserve; failure only on the last set of an isolation lift
  • Pain returns? — drop the load, run isometric protocol, retest next session
  • Failed Week 2 progression? — repeat Week 1 loads next cycle, retry the week after
  • Run day — keep separate from lifting; 30–45 min easy/moderate pace
  • Sleep — 7–9 hrs; the master recovery tool, beats every supplement
