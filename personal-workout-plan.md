## Notes

- **Warm-up (every day, 5 min)**: light cardio + dynamic stretches
- **Heavy compound warm-up sets**: empty bar × 10 → 50% × 5 → 70% × 3 → 85% × 1 → work sets
- **RIR target**: 1–2 reps in reserve; failure only on last isolation set
- **If shoulder pain returns** → drop load, run isometric protocol, retest next session
- **If you fail Week 2 progression** → stay at Week 1 loads next cycle, retry the week after
- **Volume now hits**: Quads 12 sets/wk · Hams 9 · Chest 12 · Back 16 · Shoulders 12 (all in target range)
